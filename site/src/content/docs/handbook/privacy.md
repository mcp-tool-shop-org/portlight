**Effective date:** March 23, 2026

Portlight is an offline, single-player terminal game. Your privacy is straightforward because we collect nothing.

## Data collection

Portlight does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or telemetry
- No network requests (the game runs entirely offline)
- No user accounts or registration
- No crash reporting
- No cookies or tracking

## Local data

Portlight stores game save files locally on your device in a `saves/` directory relative to where you run the game. These files never leave your machine.

## Third-party services

Portlight does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

## Children's privacy

Portlight does not collect any data from any user, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
